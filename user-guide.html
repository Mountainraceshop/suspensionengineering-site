Pressure Balance & Adjuster Authority Tool

User Guide

Purpose

This tool converts dyno force into pressure, shows how much authority your adjusters have, and helps define damping targets.

Data Required

  • Dyno force at multiple velocities (e.g. 0.1, 1.0, 2.5 m/s)
  • Adjuster-only force values
  • Total force values
  • Rod diameter or adjuster-area proxy
  • Piston or body diameter
  • Baseline pressure if known
  • Reference velocity

How It Works

Force = Area × Pressure. By dividing measured force by the correct effective area, the tool calculates pressure at different points in the system.

Adjuster Authority

The tool compares adjuster-only force to total force to calculate how much of the damping is controlled by the adjuster.

Interpreting Results

  • High adjuster % = clickers have strong influence
  • Low adjuster % = shim stack dominates
  • Out-of-band values indicate need for mechanical changes

Important

This tool is decision-support only. Always confirm with testing, rider feedback, and safety checks.
