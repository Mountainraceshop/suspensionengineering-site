Why Pressure Balance Matters

Suspension performance is controlled by pressure — but pressure is rarely measured directly. The Pressure Balance App converts real dyno force data into real internal pressures so your setup decisions are based on physics, not guesswork.

The core principle:

Force = Area × Pressure
Dyno gives us the force.
Cartridge, piston and rod geometry gives us the area.
Force ÷ Area = Real pressure at any point in the stroke.

What the App Does

  • Converts dyno force into internal pressure
  • Calculates pressure at any position in the stroke
  • Works for forks and shocks
